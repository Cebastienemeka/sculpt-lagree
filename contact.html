Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Sculpt Lagree

Do I need prior experience?
Not at all! Our Foundations class is designed for complete beginners. Our instructors guide you through every movement, ensuring you feel confident and supported from your very first class.
What should I wear?
Wear comfortable workout clothes that allow for full range of motion. Grip socks are required for safety and hygiene on the Megaformer (available for purchase at the studio).
What's the cancellation policy?
Cancel or reschedule up to 8 hours before class to avoid losing the class credit. Late cancellations and no-shows will be charged the full class amount.
Are classes safe during pregnancy?
Please consult your doctor before attending classes. Once cleared, inform your instructor so they can provide appropriate modifications for a safe and effective workout.
Is there parking available?
Yes, secure and free parking is available for all clients at our Victoria Island location. A parking attendant is on site during studio hours.
Can I book private sessions?
Absolutely! We offer 1-on-1 private sessions and duet sessions (2 people). Contact us directly to schedule a time that works for you.
How many people per class?
We maintain small class sizes with a maximum of 8 clients per session. This ensures personalized attention and proper form correction from your instructor.
Do you offer student or corporate discounts?
Yes! We offer special rates for students and corporate wellness programs. Please contact us directly for more information and to set up a corporate account.
